About Him:

Mathlete Saad from Karachi holds a bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Economics from the Institute of Business Administration. Now he brings his knowledge to the O/A-level students via well-thought-out online videos, which in turn make difficult mathematical concepts easy and fun for the whole class. His teaching approach is made to improve students' understanding and performance in mathematics.
