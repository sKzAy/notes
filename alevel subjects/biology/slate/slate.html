About Him:

Sir Saad Ziai, a passionate Biology teacher at Slate, is deeply committed to his students' success. He goes beyond traditional teaching by creating and sharing engaging YouTube videos that break down complex biological concepts into easy-to-understand lessons. His approachable and supportive style helps A/O-level students feel more confident in their studies, making a real difference in their exam preparation.
