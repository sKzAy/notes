Ahmed Bilal: Where It Finally Makes Sense!

About Him:

Sir Ahmed Bilal graduated from Imperial College London and is a famous mathematician and teacher. He was part of the National Math Olympiad team of Pakistan in 2008 and 2009 and secured top positions in many mathematics competitions. Putting his expertise to good use, Bilal now provides high-quality math tutorials on YouTube for O/A-Level students and makes advanced mathematics approachable and interactive for learners across the globe.
